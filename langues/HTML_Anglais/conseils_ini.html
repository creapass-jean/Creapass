Tips for initialization

Username

The “username” field must be filled in without spaces. You can choose to use:

  • Your name (without spaces)
  • A pseudonym
  • Any combination of characters

This username will never be requested again unless you change computers or format your disk. You must remember it.

Password

Create a strong password using this method:

1. Choose a phrase containing:

  • At least one capital letter
  • A number
  • A punctuation mark

2. Example:

“On June 18, 1940, Charles de Gaulle called on the French people to resist!”

3. Take the first letter of each word:

→ → OJ181940CdGcotFptr!

Another example: “My grandmother was born on December 15, 1950!” becomes MgwboD15,1950!

This data is not stored anywhere (neither on your computer nor in the cloud). Only you know it!
