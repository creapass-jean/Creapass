The problem with passwords

Most internet users use:

  • Very simple passwords (qwerty, 12345, etc.)
  • The same password on all websites
  • Or a combination of both

This behavior makes your personal data extremely vulnerable.

Existing solutions and their limitations

Password managers (Keepass, Dashlane, etc.) offer random passwords such as:

f&j12S+)Df@}

Problem: these passwords are stored in the cloud, potentially accessible to specialized hackers.

The Creapass solution

Creapass generates passwords:

  • On demand - No storage of your passwords
  • Locally - Data never leaves your computer
  • Reproducible - The same input always gives the same result
  • Secure - Impossible to “trace back” to the original input
