The Problem with Passwords

Most internet users use:

  • Very simple passwords (qwerty, 12345, etc.)
  • The same password on all websites
  • Or a combination of both

This behavior makes your personal data extremely vulnerable.

The Creapass Solution

Creapass generates passwords that are:

Technical Operation

Creapass uses the principle of digital fingerprinting:

  • Your credentials are irreversibly transformed
  • Same principle as serious websites (Facebook, banks...)
  • Similar technology to cryptocurrencies

Security Questions

Access to Your Computer

A particularly experienced hacker could potentially retrieve your passwords, but:

  • This is not within reach of just anyone
  • Unlikely that a real hacker would target an individual (they prefer large structures)

Ultra-Secure Solution
